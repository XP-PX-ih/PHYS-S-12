Weeks 6 and 7

One Handed Mouse/Keyboard Combo

Purpose

To allow individuals with only one useable hand to perform computer tasks at a speed similar to those with two functional hands.

Objective

To allow for decently comfortable and fast (40wpm) operation of both mouse and keyboard, simultaneously, with one hand.

Features

  • 8 Key Condensed Keyboard - ARTSEY.IO Layout
  • Modular Top for Quick Adjustability for Switching Hands
  • I/O Switch to Reverse Optical Sensor and Mouse Buttons for Switching Hands
  • Wireless Connectivity through BLE
  • Powered by Four AA Batteries
